Why Powerlifting?

Before I worked out I used to struggle with my mental health, confidence in social environments. So once I started working I gained a tremendous strength and confidence pushing myself to the limits. I keep proving to myself over and over that I'm able to get stronger not only in my lifts but as a person overall while staying Kind, Humble, and empathetic towards others. Along with gaining a gym community who supported me on my lifting journey and outside the gym.

Training

As a Competitive Powerlifter in training, my goal is to perform SBD (Squat, Bench, Deadlift) with most amount of weight while maintaining optimal form in or outside competition. My training style focuses on building maximum brute strengh through heavy loads, low repetitions (1-6 reps), and long rest periods, and supported by targeted accessory exercises.
